TEAM

E
1998

I started working with Manolo from 1998 to 2000.

2000

In 2000, I earned my Sommelier certification and did an internship at El Bulli. From El Balli, I moved to Greenhome in London, a two-star establishment with over 3,000 wine references. I stayed with Greenhome until 2003.

2003

I traveled across Europe and much of this wonderful planet, seeking inspiration and trying to confirm what I believed to be the secret to the success of the world’s best restaurants.

2004

And in 2004, I put it into practice. Essentia was born in 2004 with the help of my wife, María. In less than five years, we were awarded a Michelin star.

2018

We launched Cielo Madrid in 2018.

2019

That same year, we opened Maravilla.

2019

In 2019, Cielo earned its first Michelin star.

2020

Second Michelin star for Essentia.

All of this, without being a chef, proving that the secret to a great restaurant isn’t (only) having an outstanding chef. So, what is the secret of a great restaurant? It lies in managing talent. Selecting, training, and supporting the team. Building cohesion between the front-of-house and kitchen staff. Creating a family of individuals passionate about exceptional service and fine dining. Just like the one that will welcome you at Essentia. That is the secret of Fernando Álvarez.

Fernando Álvarez

Executive Chef

An innovative vision and a passion for culinary excellence

Fernando Álvarez, as the Executive Chef of Essentia, brings a unique vision and an innovative approach to every dish that leaves the kitchen. With years of experience in the world’s most exclusive restaurants, Fernando has perfected his craft, blending traditional techniques with modern creativity to deliver gastronomic experiences that are both memorable and surprising.

His leadership in the kitchen is reflected not only in the quality of the dishes but also in his ability to inspire and guide his team, creating a work environment where precision, passion, and respect for ingredients are always the priority. Under his direction, Essentia has become a benchmark in haute cuisine, standing out for its focus on creativity, quality, and innovation.

Carlos Díaz

Sous Chef

Carlos Díaz, as Sous Chef at Essentia, ensures every dish is a masterpiece, with a focus on precision and consistency.
